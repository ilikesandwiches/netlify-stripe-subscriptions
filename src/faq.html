Our service is about exploration and discovery. As such we work towards introducing you to 60 great fragrances each year. Our team carefully selects the fragrances each month based on a variety of factors including suitability for the season and general popularity of scent profile, among others.

We always put a mix of fragrances in each shipment to ensure you like at least one if not all of them. However, we acknowledge that part of the journey of discovery is finding things you do not like. Unfortunately, we cannot accept returns or provide refunds on used product. If there is a fragrance you are not too keen on, they make a great little gift to a friend or family member.

We aim to ship out the boxes in the first week of each month following successful payment. So, if you subscribe after the first week of the month you may not receive your box until the next shipment date. Nevertheless, we will contact you when you first subscribe to inform you of the availability of the perfumes so that you know exactly when you can expect your next shipment.
If your payment was successful and your box has not arrived by the 15th working day of the month, please contact us. If your payment was unsuccessful we cannot ship out any boxes until the payment issue is resolved.
